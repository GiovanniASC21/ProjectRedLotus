Info On Drug Addiction

Our Mission:

The objective of Project Redd Lotus is to create a place of refuge and begin the path to health for those who struggle with drug addiction. As a team we have come to recognize how difficult and scary it can be to, both, ask for help and attempt to share your story. As a result, we have actively made it our mission to bring as much ease to this process as possible
